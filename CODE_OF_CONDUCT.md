# Code of conduct

Expected behavior for everyone participating in ODTIS - specification review, GitHub, hackathons, working groups, and institutional collaboration.

## Our commitment

We are committed to a **harassment-free, professional, and inclusive** environment for people of all backgrounds. ODTIS is technical infrastructure work; debate should stay **respectful**, **evidence-based**, and focused on **interoperability and testable requirements**.

---

## Expected behavior

- Use **welcoming and inclusive** language
- Respect ** differing viewpoints** and experience levels
- Give and accept **constructive feedback** on spec text, tests, and implementations
- Focus on **what is best for the community and adopters**, not personal advantage
- **Credit others** appropriately (citations, co-authorship, conformance statements)
- Follow **ODTIS process** for normative changes ([RFC](../governance/RFC-TEMPLATE.md), not drive-by MUST edits)

---

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption
- Publishing others' **private information** without permission
- Sexual language or imagery in community spaces
- **Bad-faith** conformance claims or misrepresentation of ODTIS certification
- Retaliation against anyone reporting a concern

---

## Scope

This code applies to:

- GitHub repositories under the ODTIS / VenID stewardship
- Public review cycles and RFC comment periods
- Hackathons and events **co-branded** with ODTIS must follow the [Code of conduct](CODE-OF-CONDUCT.md).
- Official mailing lists, video calls, and conference sessions representing the project
- [Collaborate](COLLABORATE.md) activities when conducted under ODTIS branding

---

## Enforcement

Project **stewards** ([The team](TEAM.md)) are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Private warning to the individual |
| 2 | Temporary ban from community channels (GitHub, events) |
| 3 | Permanent ban for repeated or severe violations |

Stewards may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Email **[info@odtis.org](mailto:info@odtis.org?subject=ODTIS%20Code%20of%20Conduct%20report)** with subject `ODTIS CoC report`
2. Or use [GitHub private security advisory](https://github.com/odtis/core-spec/security/advisories/new) if available for sensitive cases

Reports are handled **confidentially**. We will not retaliate against good-faith reporters.
