## Editorial authority

Editors MAY:

- merge editorial fixes and errata;
- publish draft releases (`0.x.y-draft`);
- triage review cycle items in [Review Log (YAML)](REVIEW-LOG.yaml).

Editors MUST NOT:

- change a MUST without the RFC process ([RFC template](RFC-TEMPLATE.md), 14-day review);
- publish `1.0.0` without Phase 4 exit criteria ([Build plan](../PLAN-PHASES.md)).
