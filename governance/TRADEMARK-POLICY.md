## Marks

- **ODTIS** — specification name
- **ODTIS Certified** — conformance certification mark (planned)
- **VenID** — reference implementation branding ([core-impl](https://github.com/odtis/core-impl)); not the specification name
- **FinnectOS**, **Digital Trust Infrastructure** — steward / research organization branding

Spec text is CC BY 4.0; **trademarks and logos are not**.

---

## Permitted use

- Accurate reference to the specification ("implements ODTIS 0.9.0-draft")
- Academic citation per [How to cite](../publication/HOW-TO-CITE.md)

---

## Requires permission

- **ODTIS Certified** logo or mark on product marketing
- Implying foundation endorsement without L2/L3 evidence
- Modified spec marketed as "ODTIS" without editor approval
