## Editorial responsibilities

Editors **may** merge errata, publish draft releases, and triage review-cycle items.

Editors **must not** change a MUST without the [RFC process](../governance/RFC-TEMPLATE.md) (14-day review) or publish `1.0.0` without Phase 4 exit criteria.
