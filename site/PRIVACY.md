# Privacy

How the ODTIS project handles personal data on the **specification website**, **community channels**, and **collaboration** touchpoints.

<p class="odtis-hub-meta" markdown="1">

!!! note "Two privacy contexts"
    **This page** covers the ODTIS *project* (website, newsletter, events). **ODTIS section 5** normatively governs *implementations* of digital identity systems (consent, DSAR, encryption). Do not confuse them in procurement or DPIA documents.

## What we collect

| Channel | Data | Purpose | Retention |
|---------|------|---------|-----------|
| **Email** (newsletter, collaboration, feedback) | Email address, message content, name if provided | Respond, send updates you requested | Until unsubscribe or request deletion |
| **GitHub** (issues, PRs) | GitHub username, issue/PR content | Spec feedback, contributions | Per GitHub retention |
| **Site analytics** (if enabled on deploy) | Aggregated page views, referrer - no intentional PII | Improve documentation | Per analytics provider policy |
| **Hackathons / events** | Registration fields defined by co-organizer | Event logistics | Per event privacy notice |

We do **not** sell personal data. We do **not** use site data for unrelated profiling.

---

## Legal bases (illustrative)

Depending on jurisdiction and channel:

- **Consent** - newsletter subscription, optional event registration
- **Legitimate interest** - responding to specification feedback, security of services
- **Contract** - collaboration agreements with institutions

Operators implementing ODTIS MUST document their own legal bases in **policy binding statements** per jurisdiction.

---

## Your rights

Where applicable law grants them, you may request:

- **Access** to personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** when no longer needed (subject to legal/audit retention)
- **Restriction or objection** in permitted cases
- **Unsubscribe** from newsletter at any time

**How to exercise:** email [info@odtis.org](mailto:info@odtis.org?subject=ODTIS%20privacy%20request) with subject `ODTIS privacy request`.

## Cookies and local storage

The MkDocs Material site may use **local storage** for theme preference (light/dark). No advertising cookies are intentionally deployed on the ODTIS spec site. If analytics are added on production deploy, this page will be updated with cookie details.

---

## Children

ODTIS community channels are directed at professionals (implementers, researchers, operators). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
