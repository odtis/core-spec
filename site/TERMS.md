## Permitted use

You may:

- Read, download, and cite spec materials under [CC BY 4.0](LICENSE.md)
- Run conformance tests against your own deployments
- Link to ODTIS pages with accurate description of version and profile
- Participate in review cycles per [Feedback channels](../governance/FEEDBACK.md)

You may **not**:

- Misrepresent conformance or certification status
- Remove attribution or license notices from redistributed spec text
- Use ODTIS trademarks except as permitted in [Trademark policy](../governance/TRADEMARK-POLICY.md)
- Scrape or automate access in a way that degrades service for others
